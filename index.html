About Us

We are a collection of 3500 Adorable Dinos NFT collectibles on the Solana Blockchain. Each trait is hand drawn and brought together programmatically to create unique NFTs.

We have 2 variants of Dinos, namely, T-Rex and Spino. Each variant has 1500 male and 250 female dinos that would breed in future. Breeding will only happen between the same variant.

The Specs

Each Adorable Dinos is unique and algorithmically generated over 100+ possible traits. This includes headgears, eyewears, footwears, handgears, etc. These are stored in Arweave blockchain and will exist till the blockchain lives.

Road Map

Adorable dinos website/mint goes live with 3500 NFTs for 0.33 SOL each.

List on various solana NFT marketplace. Release rarity.

Creation of the Dino Dao. Dino owners will be able to take part on decision making through voting.

Breeding will be implemented where each female dino and male dino will be able to give birth to a baby dino. Each female will be eligible for multiple breeding over a period of time.
